St. Jordi 2019 (Bostod Ed.)

Saint Gorge is the patron of Catalonia (a northeast region in Spain). It is the national day of the region and it is celebrated by offering books and roses to your intimate and loved ones (more information about it on this page from Barcelona’s City council).

This is the first year outside Catalonia for this day. So, I made this super simple drawing in HTML5 and javacript for my wife, to celebrate this amazing Catalan festivity.
