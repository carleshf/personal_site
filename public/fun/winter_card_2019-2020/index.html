Winter Card 2019 - 2020

For this Christmas, I wanted to move the level of the geek christmas card further that I did before.

The previous gamified GCCs were based on a couple of smartphone games. For instance, the one for 2018-2019 was based on a game called Tomb of the Mask and the first on I gamified, 2016-2017, was based on FlappyBird.

This time I wanted to come back in time and bring back an old point-and-click game-system that I played in Where in the world is Carmen Sandiego? and Where in the space is Carmen Sandiego?.

So, for this year I codded the Who stole the Christmas from Noth Pole?. It follows the same rules as the Carmen Sandiego' games and the goal is to collect the clues to identify the thief as well as to be in the city he/she is hiding.

You can play it clicking the previous picture and the code of this GCC here.
